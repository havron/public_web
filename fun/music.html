here are some songs that I enjoy. if you would like to download them, i have made the playlist available on Spotify here. alternatively, you can run the following commands to get and listen to a local playlist of the files:
wget -r -nH -nd -np http://havron.xyz/fun/music
cd fun/music
readlink -f *.mp3 > myplaylistname.m3u
xdg-open myplaylistname.m3u

replace xdg-open with open on Mac OS X.
