Research

I am currently working as an undergraduate researcher advised by UVA Computer Science Professor David Evans in the university's computer security research group. My main research interests are in computer security and privacy problems, though I am also intrigued by areas in programming languages, human-computer interaction, and computer graphics. I plan to design and lead my own research project within computer security beginning in Summer 2016 (a link to my project page will be added in June 2016).
